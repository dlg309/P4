Cursed Knowledge

World of Jujutsu Kaisen

A compendium of the forces, history, and systems that govern the hidden war between humanity and the cursed spirits born from its own negative emotions.

Foundation

Cursed Energy

Cursed energy is negative emotion refined into a tangible force. Every human unconsciously radiates it — grief, fear, hatred — but only sorcerers can perceive and wield it. Left unchecked, cursed energy pools and solidifies into cursed spirits: manifestations of collective human dread given terrifying form.

"Cursed energy flows from negative emotions. Those who master it can reshape reality itself — or be consumed by it."

— Jujutsu High Faculty
Combat

Cursed Techniques

Inherited at birth, a cursed technique is an innate ability tied to a sorcerer's bloodline or personal nature. Each technique is unique — from Nanami Kento's precise Ratio Technique to Megumi Fushiguro's expansive Ten Shadows. Sorcerers spend years refining their technique into extensions — developed applications that push their base ability to its limits.

Ultimate Technique

Domain Expansion

The pinnacle of jujutsu combat — a sorcerer manifests their innate domain, a constructed reality overlaid on the physical world. Within a Domain, the sorcerer's cursed technique lands with guaranteed accuracy, making evasion impossible. The sheer cost in cursed energy makes Domain Expansion a high-risk gambit reserved for decisive moments.

The Three Great Clans

Three sorcerer families have dominated jujutsu society for centuries, each guardian to a legendary inherited technique.

Gojo Clan
Six Eyes · Limitless

Bearers of the Six Eyes — a rare ocular ability that perceives cursed energy with microscopic precision. Combined with the Limitless technique, the Gojo lineage produces the most overwhelmingly powerful sorcerers of any generation.

Zen'in Clan
Ten Shadows Technique

A proud and ruthless lineage that prizes technique above all else. The Ten Shadows technique summons shikigami bound to the user through a ritual ordeal — each divine beast representing terrifying combat potential.

Kamo Clan
Blood Manipulation

Masters of the Blood Manipulation technique, the Kamo clan can control their own blood as a combat medium — launching projectiles, hardening it into blades, or using it as an adhesive trap.

Institution

Jujutsu High

Tokyo and Kyoto each host a branch of Jujutsu High — the institution that trains the next generation of sorcerers. Students are ranked from Grade 4 through Grade 1, with Special Grade reserved for the most catastrophically powerful. The school operates under the authority of the Jujutsu Council, a conservative body resistant to change — and increasingly at odds with the younger generation it claims to protect.

Ranking System
Sorcerer Grades

Grade 4 through Grade 1 mark a sorcerer's assessed threat level and combat capability. Semi-Grade 1 serves as a stepping stone before the formal Grade 1 evaluation. Special Grade is not formally assigned — it is acknowledged when a sorcerer's power defies conventional measurement.

Missions
Curse Exorcism

Sorcerers are dispatched to exorcise cursed spirits that appear in the physical world. Mission difficulty corresponds to the grade of spirit encountered. Grade 1 missions frequently involve Special Grade threats and are assigned only to the most experienced sorcerers — or recklessly talented first-years.
