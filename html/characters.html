Characters

Every key figure in the jujutsu world — sorcerers, students, cursed spirits, and the forces that seek humanity's end.

Semi-Grade 1 - Special Grade Tokyo Jujutsu High
G
Satoru Gojo
Special Grade — Jujutsu High Teacher
Infinity · Limitless · Six Eyes · Unlimited Void

The strongest sorcerer alive. His Limitless technique allows him to manipulate space at an atomic level, rendering him effectively untouchable. The Six Eyes grant him near-omniscient perception of cursed energy throughout the world.

Y
Yaga Masamichi
Special Grade — Principal, Tokyo
Cursed Corpse Creation

Principal of Tokyo Jujutsu High. A stern administrator whose true technique — creating autonomous cursed corpses capable of emotion — is a closely guarded secret that the Jujutsu Council would use against him if revealed.

N
Kento Nanami
Grade 1 Sorcerer
Ratio Technique · Overtime

A former salaryman who returned to jujutsu. Nanami's Ratio Technique marks a 7:3 weak point on any object, making every strike lethal with precision. Calm, methodical, and deeply principled — the ideal mentor.

I
Mei Mei
Grade 1 Sorcerer — Mercenary
Bird Strike · Crow Manipulation

An elite Grade 1 sorcerer who freelances for the highest bidder. Her crow shikigami provide extraordinary reconnaissance and a suicide-strike technique that delivers devastating cursed energy on impact.

U
Utahime Iori
Semi-Grade 1 — Kyoto Supervisor
Solo Forbidden Area

Supervisor of Kyoto Jujutsu High and Gojo's long-suffering colleague. Her technique amplifies the output of allied sorcerers within range — a support ability that makes allied teams exponentially more lethal.

Grade 1 & Semi-Grade 1 Seasoned Field Sorcerers
H
Aoi Todo
Grade 1 — Kyoto 3rd Year
Boogie Woogie

Kyoto's strongest student — and the self-declared best friend of Yuji Itadori. His Boogie Woogie instantly swaps the position of anything imbued with cursed energy, making his movements completely unpredictable.

N
Naobito Zen'in
Grade 1 — Zen'in Clan Head
Projection Sorcery

One of the fastest sorcerers in Japan. Projection Sorcery allows him to pre-program 24 frames of movement per second — anyone he touches who cannot match the frame rate is frozen in place.

Tokyo first-years through third-years — the next generation of jujutsu sorcerers.

Tokyo — 1st Years The Core Trio
Y
Yuji Itadori
Unranked → Special Grade Vessel
Divergent Fist · Blood Manipulation · Shrine · Reverse Cursed Technique · Black Flash

The protagonist. A boy with superhuman physical ability who became the vessel for Ryomen Sukuna after swallowing a cursed finger. His optimism and raw power make him one of the most dangerous first-years in history.

M
Megumi Fushiguro
Grade 2 → Semi-Grade 1
Ten Shadows Technique · Chimera Shadow Garden Domain

Heir to the Zen'in clan's Ten Shadows technique. Megumi can summon shikigami from shadows and combine them into new forms. His Domain Expansion — Chimera Shadow Garden — transforms the battlefield into an ocean of darkness.

N
Nobara Kugisaki
Grade 3 → Grade 2
Straw Doll Technique · Hairpin · Resonance

A headstrong sorcerer from rural Japan with unshakeable pride. Her technique drives cursed nails through straw effigies to damage targets remotely — and through Resonance, she can weaponize cursed spirits' own bodies against them.

Tokyo — 2nd Years Established Sorcerers
P
Panda
Grade 2 — Mutated Cursed Corpse
Gorilla Mode · Triceratops Core

A fully autonomous cursed corpse created by Principal Yaga — one of only three in existence. Despite being non-human, Panda has developed genuine emotion, friendship, and a distinct personality. Possesses three cores with unique power states.

M
Maki Zen'in
Grade 4 → Special Grade (Post-Culling Game)
Heavenly Restriction · Weapons Mastery

Born with nearly zero cursed energy, Maki's body compensated with superhuman physical ability. Cast out by her clan, she forged herself into a weapons master. After tragedy strips away the last of her cursed energy, she transcends into something terrifying.

T
Toge Inumaki
Semi-Grade 1
Cursed Speech

Descendant of the Inumaki clan. Every word Toge speaks carries cursed energy that compels reality to obey — he can freeze, explode, or kill with a syllable. To protect those around him, he limits his speech to rice ball ingredients.

Tokyo — 3rd Years Suspended Students
K
Kinji Hakari
Grade 1 — Suspended
Idle Death Gamble · Pachislot Domain

Currently suspended due to conflict with Jujutsu management. When his Pachislot Domain hits jackpot, he becomes effectively immortal — a ceiling that places him in Special Grade territory by Gojo's own estimation.

K
Kirara Hoshi
Suspended — Tokyo 3rd Year
Love Rendezvous · Constellation Control

Suspended alongside Hakari, Kirara's technique assigns constellation symbols to targets, locking movement patterns and creating dangerous gravitational traps between marked individuals.

The adversaries of Jujutsu High — the King of Curses, Special Grade Disaster Spirits, and rogue sorcerers who abandoned any pretense of protecting the innocent.

Special Grade Cursed Spirits Disaster Curses
M
Mahito
Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Idle Transfiguration · Self Embodiment of Perfection Domain

Perhaps the most dangerous of the Disaster Curses. Mahito can reshape human souls directly — transfiguring bodies at will. He genuinely enjoys humanity's suffering, treating it as philosophical inquiry into the nature of existence.

J
Jogo
Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Maximum: Meteor · Coffin of the Iron Mountain · Reverse Cursed Technique

Born from humanity's collective fear of natural disasters. Jogo burns with volcanic ferocity — and a pride that makes him perpetually underestimate those far stronger than himself.

H
Hanami
Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Disaster Plants · Shining Sea of Flowers Domain · Reverse Cursed Technique

A Special Grade Disaster Curse born from humanity's fear of nature. Hanami seeks a world where curses coexist with the earth — a tranquil ideology made terrifying by the devastation required to achieve it.

D
Dagon
Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Disaster Water · Horizon of the Captivating Skandha · Maximum: Tsunami

Born from humanity's fear of the ocean, Dagon manipulates water and generates tsunamis on command. Calm and strategic, he is the most composed of the Disaster Curses — and no less lethal for it.

Curse Users & Rogue Sorcerers The Opposition
S
Ryomen Sukuna
Special Grade — King of Curses
Dismantle · Cleave · Malevolent Shrine · Fuga · Reverse Cursed Technique

The undisputed King of Curses. A once-human sorcerer of the ancient world whose power was so catastrophic that his twenty fingers persisted through time as cursed objects. His amusement alone is the only thing keeping his vessel alive — for now.

G
Suguru Geto
Special Grade — Curse User
Cursed Spirit Manipulation · Maximum: Uzumaki

The worst of all Curse Users. A former Special Grade sorcerer whose philosophy of a world purged of non-sorcerers led him to slaughter an entire village. Now leads a faction of cursed spirits toward that end.

K
Kenjaku
Special Grade — Ancient Sorcerer — Body Usurper
Brain Transplantation · Inherited Techniques · Reverse Cursed Technique · Domain Expansions

An ancient sorcerer surviving for over a thousand years by transplanting his brain into new host bodies. Each stolen body grants access to its owner's techniques. His true goals span centuries of patient, meticulous planning.

U
Uraume
Special Grade — Curse User
Ice Formation · Frost Calm · Reverse Cursed Technique

Sukuna's devoted attendant from the ancient era, faithfully persisting into the present. Their ice techniques freeze opponents solid and lower battlefield temperatures to lethal extremes. Loyalty to the King of Curses is absolute.

T
Toji Fushiguro
Mercenary — Sorcerer Killer
Heavenly Restriction · Physical Prowess · Weapons Mastery

Born with zero cursed energy in exchange for immense physical strength — an anomaly that transformed him into something inhuman. With a physique elevated far beyond ordinary limits, Toji became a mercenary who hunted sorcerers for sport and profit.
